Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

StainRol ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit or order from this website, and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Contact and delivery details you provide at checkout (name, phone number, address).
  • Order details, such as items purchased and payment method (UPI or Cash on Delivery).
  • Basic device and browsing data collected automatically, such as pages visited and referring links.

How we use your information

  • To process, pack, and deliver your order.
  • To contact you about your order status, if needed.
  • To improve our website and product offering.

Analytics and advertising

We use tools such as Meta (Facebook) Pixel and Snapchat Pixel to understand how visitors use our site and to measure the performance of our ads. These tools may collect information about your visit but do not receive your name, address, or phone number.

Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We share delivery details only with the courier partner needed to fulfil your order, and payment details only with our payment processor (Razorpay) when you pay via UPI.

Data retention

We retain order information for as long as necessary to fulfil orders, handle returns, and meet legal or accounting requirements.

Your rights

You may contact us to ask what information we hold about you, or to request that it be corrected or deleted, subject to any orders still in progress.

Contact us

For any privacy-related questions, please email us at adfexmedia@gmail.com.
