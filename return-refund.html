Return & Refund Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

We want you to be happy with your StainRol purchase. If something isn't right, here's how returns and refunds work.

Return window

You may request a return within 7 days of delivery if the product is damaged, defective, or significantly different from what was ordered.

How to request a return

  • Contact us using the details on your order confirmation, including your order number and a photo of the issue.
  • We'll review your request and confirm the next steps, typically within 1–2 business days.

Condition for returns

  • The product should be unused and in its original packaging, where possible.
  • Returns due to damage or defect are accepted regardless of packaging condition.

Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we'll notify you of the approval status. Approved refunds are processed to your original payment method (or bank transfer for Cash on Delivery orders) within 5–7 business days.

Non-returnable situations

We're unable to accept returns for change-of-mind requests once the product has been used, or for issues reported after the 7-day window.

Questions

If you have any questions about your order, delivery, or a return, please email us at adfexmedia@gmail.com.
